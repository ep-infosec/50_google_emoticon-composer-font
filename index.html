What is this?

This is a prototype of a composable glyph font to create composable expressions. You can get started by typing in your initials to see what it reveals :) Uppercase letters are eyes, lower case letters are mouths and try the numbers out for a surprise.

You are welcome to copy and paste your characters; however, as this is a non-standard font, it won't render anywhere else for now. We recommend you take a screenshot ;0)
